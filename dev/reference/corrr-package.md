# corrr: Correlations in R

A tool for exploring correlations. It makes it possible to easily
perform routine tasks when exploring correlation matrices such as
ignoring the diagonal, focusing on the correlations of certain variables
against others, or rearranging and visualizing the matrix in terms of
the strength of the correlations.
